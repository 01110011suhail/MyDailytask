Angela's Reciepe For the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1/2 cup warmmilk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1egg york
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. after the dough has doubled in the size roll it out into large rectangle
  7. cut eqaully and roll it out
